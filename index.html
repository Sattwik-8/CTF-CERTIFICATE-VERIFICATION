What is 0xDAY CTF?

A 6-day cybersecurity event organized by IEEE HIT Student Branch. 4 days of hands-on training followed by an online and offline Capture The Flag competition. Built for beginners. Designed for hackers.

What will you receive?

Three distinct certificates — each embedded with a unique ID and QR code for instant verification.

APT

APPRENTICESHIP CERTIFICATE

Training Certificate

Awarded to every attendee who completed the 4-day workshop. Recognises your commitment to learning cybersecurity fundamentals.

ID FORMAT APT-XXXXX
